How LTV is Calculated

The lifetime value uses a perpetuity formula for recurring subscription revenue with churn.
LTV = (Revenue − Delivery Cost) ÷ (1 − Renewal Rate) − CAC
LTV = ($40 − $2) ÷ (1 − 0.80) − $15
LTV = $38 ÷ 0.20 − $15 = $190 − $15 = $175
  • 1
    Net revenue per year
    Revenue minus the cost to actually serve the customer each year.
    $40 − $2 = $38/year
  • 2
    Expected lifetime revenue (perpetuity)
    With 80% renewal, the average customer stays ~5 years. Divide net revenue by churn rate (1 − renewal rate) to get lifetime gross value.
    $38 ÷ 0.20 = $190
  • 3
    Subtract acquisition cost
    The one-time cost to get this customer in the door.
    $190 − $15 = $175
